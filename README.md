# 📊 Sales Analysis Dashboard — Microsoft Excel

## 🗂️ Project Overview

An interactive **Sales Analysis Dashboard** built entirely in Microsoft Excel for a retail company operating across multiple US cities and states (2014–2017). The project analyzes sales performance, profitability, product rankings, and geographic distribution — delivered through **2 interactive dashboards** with slicers and page navigation.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered

- What are the total sales, profit, and order counts across 4 years?
- Which customer segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office) drives the most revenue and profit?
- Which product category has the highest sales vs profit ratio?
- What are the Top 10 and Bottom 10 products by sales?
- How do sales trend across quarters?
- Which US states and cities generate the most revenue?

---

## 🛠️ Work Done

### Pivot Tables
Built multiple Pivot Tables to power all dashboard visuals:
- Sales & Profit by Segment
- Sales & Profit by Category (Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology)
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Bottom 10 Products by Sales
- Sales by Quarter (Qtr1 → Qtr4)
- Sales by State (geographic breakdown)

### Dashboard Design
- **2 fully interactive dashboards** built from Pivot Charts
- **Slicers** for: Year (2014–2017), Quarter (Qtr1–Qtr4), City
- **Page Navigation buttons** to switch between dashboards
- Clean dark-themed UI with consistent color scheme

**Visuals:**

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- **Consumer segment** dominates both sales (50.5%) and profit — primary target for retention campaigns.
- **Technology** is the highest revenue category ($836K) with a healthy profit margin ($145K).
- **Furniture** has the worst profit ratio — $742K in sales but only $18K in profit — a major margin issue.
- **Q4 consistently outperforms** all quarters at $878K — seasonal demand pattern.
- **16% average discount** is high and likely contributing to Furniture's low profitability.
- **Texas** is the top-performing state by revenue in the western region.
